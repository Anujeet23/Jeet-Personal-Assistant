Ingredients:

-2 lbs. skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
-1/4 cup butter, melted
-1 large onion, diced
-1 tablespoon minced garlic
-1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
-2 teaspoons ground coriander
-2 teaspoons garam masala
-1 teaspoon ground cumin
-1 teaspoon ground turmeric
-1 teaspoon paprika
-1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
-1 (14.5 ounce) can tomato sauce
-1/2 cup heavy cream
-1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

2. Place chicken pieces in a single layer in a shallow baking dish. Pour melted butter over chicken. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and lightly browned.

3. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger; cook and stir until onion is softened and lightly browned. Stir in coriander, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, paprika, and cayenne pepper
